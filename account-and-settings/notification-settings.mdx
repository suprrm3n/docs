# Notification Settings

Tavrn notifies you when things happen that matter to you. Here's how to tune that to the right level, not so quiet that you miss things, not so loud that it's noise.

---

## Where to Find Notification Settings

**Settings → Notifications**, everything's in there.

---

## The Two Notification Toggles

The **Notifications** settings tab has two main controls:

### Push Notifications
Receive notifications when you're not actively using Tavrn, in your browser or on your device. This is the main toggle for being pinged about incoming DMs, friend requests, and mentions.

### Email Summaries (Digest)
Get a digest of missed activity sent to your email. When enabled, you can choose:
- **Daily** or **Weekly** frequency
- What to include: unread DMs, room mentions & pings, friend requests & activity

This is great if you want a roundup without being interrupted in real time.

## In-App Notifications

The **bell icon** (🔔) in the sidebar is your notification center. A red dot means you have unread notifications.

Click it to open the notifications panel where you'll see:
- All recent notifications
- Who they're from (with a link to their profile)
- What they're about
- When they arrived

From the panel, you can **click a notification** to navigate to the relevant conversation or person.

---

## Push Notifications

Tavrn supports **push notifications** to your browser or device via OneSignal. These let you know about activity even when you don't have Tavrn open.

**Enabling push notifications:**
1. The first time you use Tavrn, your browser will ask if you want to allow notifications, click **Allow**
2. If you blocked it, you can re-enable from your browser settings (click the 🔒 lock icon in your address bar → Notifications → Allow)

Push notifications work when:
- You're logged into Tavrn but the tab isn't active
- You have Tavrn open in the background
- On mobile, when you've added Tavrn to your home screen

## Notification Badges

Unread notification counts show as badges:
- On the **bell icon** in the sidebar
- In the **browser tab** title (e.g., `(3) Tavrn`)
- On the **app icon** if installed to home screen on mobile

## Common Notification Setups

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Real-time everything">
    - Push notifications: Enabled
    - Email summaries: Off
    - Perfect for: active community members, room admins/mods who need to stay on top of things
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Real-time + weekly recap">
    - Push notifications: Enabled
    - Email summaries: Weekly
    - Perfect for: people who want live pings but also a weekly summary of what they missed
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Email-only, no interruptions">
    - Push notifications: Disabled
    - Email summaries: Daily or Weekly
    - Perfect for: casual users who prefer to check in on their own schedule
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

---

## Notification Privacy

Notifications on Tavrn show your activity to you, not to others. Nobody can see your notification settings or know whether you've read their message based on your notification configuration.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I'm not getting any notifications">
    Check your browser's permission settings for Tavrn, notifications need to be "Allowed" at the browser level. Click the 🔒 lock in your address bar and confirm notifications are set to Allow. Also verify Push Notifications is toggled on in **Settings → Notifications**.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I'm getting too many notifications, how do I reduce them?">
    Toggle off Push Notifications in **Settings → Notifications** to stop real-time pings. Switch to email summaries if you still want a periodic roundup.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do notifications work when my phone is locked?">
    If you've added Tavrn to your home screen (as a PWA) and have notifications enabled, yes, you can receive lock screen notifications on mobile. This depends on your device and browser's support for web push.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
