</Tip>

## Payment & Billing

Tavrn uses **Stripe** for payment processing, the same secure payment infrastructure used by millions of businesses worldwide.

**Accepted payment methods:**
- Credit / debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover)
- Other methods may be available depending on your region

Your payment info is handled entirely by Stripe and is never stored directly by Tavrn.

---

## When Billing Happens

- **Monthly:** Charged on the same date each month from when you subscribed
- **Yearly:** Charged once, then again one year later (unless you cancel before renewal)

You'll receive an email receipt after each billing event.

---

## Upgrading from Monthly to Yearly

If you start on monthly and want to switch to yearly:
1. Go to **Settings → Cocoa**
2. Look for the option to change your billing cycle
3. You'll be prorated, credited for the remaining time on your monthly plan before the yearly charge kicks in

---

## Canceling

You can cancel your Cocoa subscription at any time:
1. **Settings → Cocoa → Cancel Subscription**
2. Confirm cancellation

You keep your Cocoa perks until the **end of the current billing period**, you're not cut off mid-month or mid-year.

<Note>
  Canceling means your subscription won't renew. It doesn't issue a refund for time already paid. If you cancel a yearly plan with 6 months remaining, you keep the perks for those 6 months.
</Note>
