# Receiving Gifted Cocoa 🎁

Someone gifted you Cocoa, that's a genuinely kind thing. Here's what happens next.

---

## What "Gifted Cocoa" Means

When a Tavrn admin or team member gifts Cocoa to your account, your subscription is activated automatically. No payment required on your end, no codes to enter, it just shows up.

You'll receive an **in-app notification** letting you know that you've received Cocoa, along with how long it's active for.

---

## Your Perks Are Active Immediately

As soon as Cocoa is applied to your account, all perks unlock right away:

- **Animated avatar**, Upload a GIF as your profile picture
- **Cocoa badge**, Appears on your profile
- **HD media uploads**, Higher quality images and larger file size limits
- **Rich custom status**, Emoji + text status
- **Profile themes**, Exclusive visual themes
- **Extended message history**, Unlimited message history

[Full perks breakdown →](/cocoa-premium/cocoa-perks-explained)

---

## How Long Does It Last?

The notification you receive will tell you how long your gifted Cocoa lasts. When the period ends:
- Your perks deactivate
- Your profile returns to the standard free experience
- Nothing is deleted, your history, messages, and connections stay intact

---

## What If I Already Have a Subscription?

If you're already a paying Cocoa subscriber, gifted time **stacks on top** of your existing subscription, it doesn't replace or shorten what you've already paid for.

---

## The Cocoa Badge

While gifted Cocoa is active, your profile shows the same **☕ Cocoa badge** as any other Cocoa member. There's no distinction between gifted and paid, you get the full experience.

---

## Keeping Your Cocoa Going

Gifted Cocoa expires when the gift period ends. If you want to keep your perks after that:

1. Go to **Settings → Cocoa**
2. Subscribe to a monthly or yearly plan
3. Your perks continue without any gap

[Learn about Cocoa plans →](/cocoa-premium/cocoa-plans-and-pricing)
