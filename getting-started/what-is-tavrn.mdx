# Welcome to Tavrn 🔥

Tavrn is a **community platform built around real connection**, not algorithms, not engagement metrics, not ads. Think of it like a warm, cozy gathering place online where you can chat with friends, hang out in communities, hop on voice calls, and actually enjoy spending time on the internet again.

<Note>
  The name "Tavrn" is intentional, it evokes a tavern, a classic gathering spot where people come together, share stories, and build relationships. That's exactly the vibe we're going for.
</Note>

## The Big Idea

Here's the deal: most social platforms are designed to keep you scrolling. They show you content calculated to maximize the time you spend staring at a screen, not content that actually makes you happy or helps you connect with people you care about.

**Tavrn is the opposite of that.**

There's no algorithm deciding what you see. Your messages appear in the order they were sent. Your communities are *yours*, not curated by a system trying to monetize your attention. Just real conversations, real communities, real connection.

## How is Tavrn Different from Discord?

We know what you're thinking. A lot of people come to Tavrn from Discord, so here's a quick breakdown:

| Feature | Tavrn | Discord |
|---|---|---|
| Algorithm-driven feed | ❌ Never | Sometimes |
| Rooms & channels | ✅ Yes | ✅ Yes |
| Voice & video calls | ✅ Yes | ✅ Yes |
| Premium perks | Cosmetic + quality-of-life | Mostly cosmetic |
| Privacy settings | Very granular | Basic |
| Warm, cozy vibes | 🔥 Absolutely | Debatable |

<Tip>
  Tavrn isn't trying to be "Discord but worse." It's trying to be something better, a place that genuinely prioritizes the people using it.
</Tip>

## Is Tavrn Free?

**Yes, completely.** The core Tavrn experience, messaging, rooms, voice calls, everything, is totally free and always will be.

There *is* an optional premium membership called **Cocoa** that adds some nice extras (like animated avatars, higher quality media uploads, and exclusive profile customizations), but nothing essential is locked behind it.

<Info>
  Want to know more about Cocoa? Check out the [What is Cocoa?](/cocoa-premium/what-is-cocoa) article.
</Info>

## Who is Tavrn For?

Honestly? Anyone. But Tavrn tends to really click for:

- **Gamers** who want a chill place to hang out between sessions
- **Friend groups** who want a private space to stay in touch
- **Hobbyist communities** built around anything from art to tabletop RPGs
- **People tired of Big Social Media** and craving something more human
- **Creators** who want to build an engaged community without the algorithm tax
