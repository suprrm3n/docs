# Adding Your First Friends

Tavrn is way more fun when you've got people to hang out with. Here's how to find friends and get your social circle going.

---

## How the Friend System Works

Friendships on Tavrn are **mutual**, you send a request, the other person accepts it, and you're friends. It's a two-way relationship.

Once you're friends with someone, you can:
- Start a direct message conversation anytime
- See their online/offline status
- View their full profile (depending on their privacy settings)
- Initiate voice or video calls

---

## Finding People to Add

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Search by Username">
    The quickest way to add someone you already know.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Go to the Friends section">
        Click **Friends** in the sidebar.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Click 'Add Friend'">
        This opens the user search.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Type their username">
        Search by `@username`. You need the exact username, it's case-insensitive but needs to be accurate.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Send the request">
        When their profile appears, click **Add Friend**. They'll get a notification and can accept or decline.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="From a Room">
    Met someone cool in a room? You can add them right from the chat.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Click on their username or avatar">
        This opens their **mini profile**, a small popup with their info.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Click 'Add Friend'">
        The request goes out instantly.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="From a DM">
    If someone's already messaged you (or you them), you can friend them from the conversation.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open the conversation">
        Head to the DM in your sidebar.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Click their name at the top">
        This opens their profile or mini profile.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Click 'Add Friend'">
        Done.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Managing Friend Requests

All your pending friend requests live in the **Friends** section of the sidebar, under the **Pending** tab.

From there you can:
- **Accept** a request to become friends
- **Decline** a request (they won't be notified)
- **See your outgoing requests** to check if someone's accepted yours yet

<Note>
  The person whose request you decline won't receive a notification saying "your request was declined." It just silently doesn't go through from their perspective.
</Note>

## Removing a Friend

People grow apart, it happens. To remove someone from your friends list:

1. Open the **Friends** section
2. Find their name in the list
3. Right-click (or tap and hold on mobile) → **Remove Friend**

Removed friends won't be notified. You'll no longer see each other's status, and you'll need to send a new friend request if you want to reconnect.

---

## Blocking Someone

Blocking is more powerful than removing. When you block someone:
- They can't send you friend requests
- They can't message you
- You won't see each other's activity

To block someone: click their profile → **Block User**.

<Info>
  For a full breakdown of blocking and its effects, see [Blocking Someone](/profile-and-social/blocking-someone).
</Info>

## Privacy: Who Can Send You Friend Requests?

By default, anyone on Tavrn can send you a friend request. But you can change this.

In **Settings → Privacy & Safety**, you can set friend request permissions to:
- **Everyone** (default)
- **Friends of friends**, Only people who share mutual friends with you
- **Nobody**, Lock it down completely

This is useful if you're a public figure or just prefer a more curated friends list.

## The Tavrn Welcome Bot

When you first sign up, you'll automatically be friended by the **Tavrn** account, that's us! It's how we send you tips, announcements, and the occasional update about new features. You can mute or leave that conversation anytime without it affecting anything.

## Your Friends List

Once you've got some friends, they'll all show up in the **Friends** section, sorted by online status. You can see at a glance who's online, who's away, and who's offline.

Click anyone's name to:
- Open a DM
- See their profile
- Start a call

<Tip>
  Friends with a 🟢 green dot are online. 🟡 Yellow means away or idle. 🔴 Red means busy (do not disturb). ⚫ Grey means offline or invisible.
</Tip>
