# Finding & Joining Rooms

Rooms are where Tavrn really comes alive. They're community spaces organized around shared interests, and finding the right ones makes all the difference.

---

## What's a Room, Exactly?

A room is a dedicated space for a community. Inside, you'll find:

- **Text channels**, For ongoing conversations organized by topic
- **Voice channels**, For hanging out and talking in real time
- **Members**, Other people in the community
- **Roles**, Different permission levels (more on that in [Roles & Permissions](/rooms-and-communities/roles-and-permissions))

Think of it like a clubhouse that's always open.

---

## Three Ways to Join a Room

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Via Invite Link">
    **The most common way.** If someone shares a Tavrn invite link with you, just click it.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Click the invite link">
        It'll open a preview showing the room's name, description, and member count.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Click 'Join Room'">
        That's it! You're in. The room will appear in your room list immediately.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    Invite links look like: `tavrn.app/invite/xyzabc`
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Via Invite Code">
    If someone gives you an invite **code** (not a full link), you can enter it manually.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open the 'Join Room' dialog">
        Click the **+** icon in your room list and choose **Join a Room**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enter the invite code">
        Paste in the code and hit **Join**.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Room Discovery">
    Browse public rooms directly within Tavrn.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Click the 'Discover' or 'Explore Rooms' button">
        This opens the room discovery page where you can browse and search.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Browse or search">
        Filter by category, search by keyword, or browse featured communities.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Click a room and join">
        Preview the room's info and click **Join**.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Note>
      Only rooms that have been made **public** or are **AHA Verified** appear in discovery. Most small, private rooms won't show up here.
    </Note>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## AHA Verified Rooms

The **AHA program** is Tavrn's verification system for quality communities. AHA Verified rooms have been reviewed and meet certain standards for activity, organization, and community health.

Look for the ✅ AHA badge when browsing, it's a good signal that a room is established and well-run.

<Info>
  Running a room and want to apply for AHA verification? Check out [AHA Verified Rooms](/rooms-and-communities/aha-verified-rooms).
</Info>

## After You Join

Once you're in a room, a few things happen:

1. The room icon appears in your left sidebar
2. You'll be given the **Member** role by default
3. You can start chatting in text channels right away
4. Voice channels are available to hop into
5. The room's members list is visible on the right

<Tip>
  Most rooms have a **#rules** or **#welcome** channel at the top. Read it before diving in, it'll tell you what the community is about and any expectations they have.
</Tip>

---

## Leaving a Room

Changed your mind or just cleaning up your room list? You can leave anytime.

1. Right-click the room icon in your sidebar (or long-press on mobile)
2. Select **Leave Room**
3. Confirm

<Warning>
  If you're the **owner** of a room, you can't just leave, you'd need to transfer ownership first, or delete the room entirely.
</Warning>

---

## How Many Rooms Can I Join?

There's no hard limit on the number of rooms you can join. Join as many or as few as you want, your room list is yours to manage.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I preview a room before joining?">
    Yes! When you click an invite link, you'll see a preview with the room's name, description, and member count before you commit to joining.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can a room kick or ban me?">
    Yes. Room admins and moderators can kick or ban members. If you're banned, you won't be able to rejoin via the same invite link.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if I was banned by mistake?">
    Bans are managed at the room level by that room's admins. Reach out to a room admin directly (if possible) to appeal. Tavrn support can't intervene in individual room moderation decisions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I can't find the room I'm looking for">
    If a room is private, it won't show up in discovery and can only be accessed via invite. Ask the room owner for an invite link if you know it exists.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
