# Setting Up Your Profile

Your Tavrn profile is how the rest of the community gets to know you. It doesn't have to be a masterpiece on day one, but taking a few minutes to fill it out makes a huge difference in how people connect with you.

---

## The Profile Setup Flow

When you first sign up, Tavrn walks you through a quick profile setup. You can also update any of this anytime through **Settings → Profile**.

### What You Can Set

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="🖼️ Avatar" icon="image">
    Upload a photo or image that represents you. Square images work best. Cocoa members can use animated GIFs.
  </Card>
  <Card title="🏷️ Display Name" icon="tag">
    This is the name shown in chats and on your profile, it's separate from your username and can be anything.
  </Card>
  <Card title="✍️ Bio" icon="align-left">
    A short blurb about yourself. What are you into? What kind of vibes do you bring? Keep it brief but real.
  </Card>
  <Card title="🎨 Banner Color" icon="palette">
    Pick a hex color for your profile banner, the colored strip at the top of your profile card.
  </Card>
  <Card title="📍 Location" icon="map-pin">
    Optional. Share your city or region if you want people to know where you're from.
  </Card>
  <Card title="🔗 Social Links" icon="link">
    Link out to your other profiles, Twitter, Instagram, YouTube, personal site, whatever you want.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Pronouns

Tavrn has a dedicated pronouns field on every profile. It's right there, prominent, because we believe everyone deserves to be addressed correctly without having to explain themselves.

You can set your pronouns to anything, `she/her`, `he/him`, `they/them`, `any/all`, or something totally custom. It'll be displayed publicly on your profile.

<Tip>
  Pronouns are optional. Fill them in if you'd like people to know, leave it blank if you prefer.
</Tip>

## Your Username vs. Your Display Name

A lot of people get confused about this, here's how it works:

| | Username | Display Name |
|---|---|---|
| **What it does** | Unique identifier for your account | The name shown in chats |
| **Can be changed** | Yes (with care) | Freely, anytime |
| **Used for @mentions** | ✅ Yes | ❌ No |
| **Must be unique** | ✅ Yes | ❌ No |
| **Example** | `@coffee_witch` | `Margaux ☕` |

<Note>
  When someone @mentions you, they use your **username**. Your display name is just what shows up in conversation, it's more for personality than identity.
</Note>

## Uploading an Avatar

Your avatar shows up everywhere, DMs, room chats, your profile, friend lists. Make it something that feels like you.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Profile">
    Open the settings menu and navigate to your profile page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click your current avatar">
    This opens the file picker. Select an image from your device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm and save">
    Your avatar will upload and update instantly. No page refresh needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Supported formats:** JPEG, PNG, WebP, GIF  
**Maximum file size:** 25MB

<Info>
  Want an **animated GIF avatar**? That's a Cocoa perk! Check out [Animated Avatars](/cocoa-premium/animated-avatars) to learn more.
</Info>

## Choosing Your Banner Color

The banner is that colored strip at the top of your profile card. It's a small detail, but it makes your profile feel distinctly *yours*.

To change it:
1. Go to **Settings → Profile**
2. Find the **Banner Color** field
3. Enter a valid hex code (like `#ff6a33` for Tavrn's signature orange)
4. Save your changes

<Tip>
  Don't know your hex codes off the top of your head? Tools like [coolors.co](https://coolors.co) or [htmlcolorcodes.com](https://htmlcolorcodes.com) make it easy to find the perfect color.
</Tip>

## Adding Social Links

You can link your other profiles in your Tavrn bio. These show up as clickable icons on your profile card. Currently supported platforms include:

- Twitter / X
- Instagram
- YouTube
- Twitch
- GitHub
- Personal website

To add them, go to **Settings → Profile → Social Links** and paste in your URLs.

## Your Profile Visibility

By default, your profile is visible to everyone on Tavrn. But you can lock it down if you prefer:

- **Public**, Anyone can see your profile
- **Friends only**, Only people on your friends list can see full profile details
- **Private**, Profile details are hidden from everyone

This is configured in **Settings → Privacy & Safety**. See [Profile Visibility](/privacy-and-safety/profile-visibility) for a full breakdown.

## Profile Tips & Ideas

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What should I write in my bio?">
    Think of your bio as a tiny introduction. Some things that work well: your main interests or hobbies, something funny or quirky about yourself, what kinds of communities or rooms you're in. You don't need to write an essay, a couple of lines is perfect.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What makes a good avatar?">
    Something recognizable even at small sizes works best. A clear face photo, a distinctive illustration, a logo, or even just a bold icon works great. Avoid images that are too busy or complex, they'll just look like noise at 40px.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I change my avatar and profile frequently?">
    Absolutely! Your display name, avatar, bio, and banner are all freely changeable whenever you want. Switch it up for different vibes, seasons, or just because you feel like it.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do other people get notified when I update my profile?">
    No. Profile updates are silent, nobody gets pinged when you change your avatar or bio.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
