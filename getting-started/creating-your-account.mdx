# Creating Your Account

Signing up for Tavrn is fast, free, and painless. Here's everything you need to know.

---

## Before You Start

You'll need:
- A valid email address
- A password you'll actually remember
- About 2 minutes of your time

---

## The Sign-Up Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Tavrn homepage">
    Open Tavrn in your browser and click the **Sign Up** button. You'll be taken to the registration page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email address">
    Use an email you have access to. You'll need it to verify your account and for password resets down the line.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a strong password">
    Pick something you haven't used elsewhere. A mix of letters, numbers, and symbols is always a good idea.
    <Tip>
      Tavrn supports multi-factor authentication (MFA). We strongly recommend enabling it after you sign up for extra security.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from Tavrn. Click the link inside to confirm your address.
    <Note>
      Don't see it? Check your spam or junk folder. Still nothing after a few minutes? You can request a new verification email from the login page.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your profile">
    Once verified, you'll be walked through the profile setup, choose your username, add a photo, and write a short bio. This is covered in depth in [Setting Up Your Profile](/getting-started/setting-up-your-profile).
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Choosing Your Username

Your username is how other people find and mention you on Tavrn. A few things to know:

- Usernames must be **unique** across the entire platform
- They're case-sensitive in display but matched case-insensitively when searching
- You can change your display name without changing your username
- Use `@username` to mention people in messages

<Warning>
  Choose your username thoughtfully! While you can update your display name freely, changing your username may affect how existing friends and communities recognize you.
</Warning>

## Signing In with Steam

Prefer not to use email? Tavrn supports **Steam login**, you can authenticate using your existing Steam account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="On the login page, click 'Sign in with Steam'">
    You'll be redirected to Steam's login page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize Tavrn">
    Steam will ask you to confirm that you want to give Tavrn access. Click **Authorize**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete your profile">
    After your first Steam login, you'll be prompted to set up your Tavrn username and profile, just like with email signup.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Learn more about Steam login in the [Steam Login](/account-and-settings/steam-login) article.
</Info>

## Already Have an Account?

If you're returning to Tavrn or set up a second account before, just hit **Log In** instead of Sign Up and enter your credentials.

Tavrn also supports **multiple accounts**, you can switch between them without logging out. Check out [Managing Multiple Accounts](/account-and-settings/managing-multiple-accounts) for details.

## Troubleshooting Sign-Up Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I didn't receive a verification email">
    First, check your spam/junk folder. Email filters sometimes catch automated messages. If it's not there, wait a few minutes and try requesting a new verification email from the login screen. Still nothing? Make sure you entered your email address correctly during sign-up.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="My username is already taken">
    Usernames on Tavrn are unique across the whole platform. Try adding numbers, underscores, or a short suffix to make yours stand out. For example, if `alex` is taken, try `alex_`, `alex42`, or `im_alex`.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I forgot my password before I even logged in">
    Head to the login page and click **Forgot Password**. You'll receive a reset link via email. If you signed up with Steam, you'll use Steam to log in instead of a password.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The sign-up page isn't loading">
    Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. If the issue persists, try a different browser or check if there's a known outage on Tavrn's status page.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
