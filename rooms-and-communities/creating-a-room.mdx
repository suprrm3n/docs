# Creating a Room

So you want to build a community. Whether it's a private hangout for your friend group or an open community around a topic you love, creating a room on Tavrn is free and takes about two minutes.

---

## Creating Your First Room

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click the + button in your room list">
    In the left sidebar, you'll see your list of joined rooms. At the bottom, there's a **+** button. Click it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose 'Create a Room'">
    You'll see options to create a new room or join an existing one. Choose **Create**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name your room">
    Give it a name that represents your community. This can always be changed later.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add an icon (optional but recommended)">
    Upload an image for your room icon. Square images work best, it'll be cropped to a circle in most views.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write a description (optional)">
    A sentence or two about what your room is for. This shows up on invite previews and in room discovery if your room is public.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create!">
    Hit **Create Room**. Your room is live instantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## After Creation: Your Default Setup

When you create a room, Tavrn sets you up with some basic defaults:
- A **#general** text channel
- A **General Voice** channel
- You're automatically set as the **Owner**

From here, it's all yours to customize.

---

## Setting Up Your Room

Once your room is created, here's what to do next:

### 1. Create Your Channels

Think about what conversations you want to have and create channels for them. A few tips:

- **Start small**, You don't need 30 channels on day one. A few well-named channels are better than a maze of specific ones.
- **Add categories**, Group related channels with category headers
- **Create a #rules or #welcome channel**, Helps onboard new members

To create a channel:
1. Hover over a category header (or the channel list) and click the **+** icon
2. Choose **Text Channel** or **Voice Channel**
3. Name it and confirm

<Tip>
  Channel names can only use lowercase letters, numbers, and hyphens. No spaces, use hyphens instead: `#game-night`, not `#Game Night`.
</Tip>

### 2. Invite Your First Members

Share your room invite link with the people you want to join. To get your invite link:
1. Open **Room Settings**
2. Go to **Invites**
3. Create or copy an invite link

Invite links can be set to expire after a certain number of uses or time period.

<Info>
  For more on invites, see [Inviting Members](/rooms-and-communities/inviting-members).
</Info>

### 3. Customize Your Icon & Banner

A recognizable room icon goes a long way. Upload something that represents the vibe of your community, a logo, an illustration, a meme, whatever fits.

## Room Visibility Settings

You control whether your room is public or private:

- **Private** (default), Only people with an invite link can join
- **Public**, Your room can be discovered through Tavrn's room browser

To change this, go to **Room Settings → Overview** and adjust the visibility setting.

<Warning>
  Think carefully before making your room public. Once you do, random people can find and join it. Make sure your rules and moderation setup are ready.
</Warning>

---

## Organizing Channels with Categories

Categories are the collapsible folders that group your channels. Here's how to create one:

1. Click the **+** next to "Categories" in your channel management settings (or right-click in the channel list)
2. Name the category
3. Drag channels into it

Example categories for a gaming room: `WELCOME`, `GENERAL`, `GAMES`, `VOICE`, `BOT COMMANDS`

---

## Room Limits

- You can create multiple rooms, no hard limit
- Each room can have many channels and categories
- Channel positions can be manually reordered by dragging

---

## Growing Your Community

Starting a room is the easy part, here are a few ideas for getting it going:

- **Invite your existing friends first**, Build a core group before going public
- **Post in related communities**, If there's a related subreddit, Discord, or community, let them know Tavrn exists
- **Apply for AHA verification**, Once your room is established, AHA verification can boost your visibility. [AHA Verified Rooms →](/rooms-and-communities/aha-verified-rooms)
- **Be active yourself**, Nothing kills a community faster than an absent owner. Show up, start conversations, welcome new members.

---

## Transferring or Deleting a Room

**To transfer ownership** to another member:
- Room Settings → Members → Click a member → Transfer Ownership

**To delete a room:**
- Room Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Room
- This is permanent and requires confirmation

<Warning>
  Deleting a room is **irreversible**. All messages, channels, and data are permanently removed. Make absolutely sure before proceeding.
</Warning>
