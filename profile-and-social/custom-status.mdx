# Custom Status

Your custom status is the little message that shows up on your profile and in friend lists, right next to your status indicator. It's a lightweight way to tell people what you're up to without having to respond to "what are you doing?" from 12 different people.

---

## What a Custom Status Looks Like

Your custom status appears:

- **On your mini profile**, when someone clicks your name in a chat
- **In the friends list**, visible to anyone who has you added
- **On your profile page**, visible based on your privacy settings

A custom status looks something like:
```
🎮 Currently: Elden Ring (send help)
```
or
```
📝 Writing something. Back soon.
```
or even just
```
💀
```

(We've all been there.)

---

## Setting a Custom Status

1. Click the **status dot** next to your avatar at the bottom of the sidebar
2. Click **Set Custom Status** (or the text input that appears)
3. Type your status, you can include emoji!
4. Save / confirm

Your status updates immediately for anyone who can see your presence.

---

## Using Emoji in Your Status

Emoji are first-class citizens in custom status. Type or paste any emoji at the start (or anywhere in) your status text.

A few formats people like:
- `🎵 listening to: [whatever you're playing]`
- `☕ definitely on my [nth] cup of coffee`
- `🌙 late night mode activated`
- `🚀 building things`
- `🤫 lurking`

You can also use emoji shortcodes if you know them, `:coffee:` → ☕

---

## Clearing Your Status

To remove your custom status:
1. Click the status dot
2. Click on your current custom status
3. Delete the text and save (or click a "Clear" button if one's present)

Alternatively, just replace it with a new one.

## Custom Status vs. Online Status

Custom status and your online status (Online, Away, Busy, Offline) are separate things that work together:

| | Online Status | Custom Status |
|---|---|---|
| **What it shows** | Green/yellow/red/grey dot | Text message |
| **What it means** | Whether you're active/available | What you're doing or feeling |
| **How to set it** | Click status dot → pick a state | Click status dot → type text |
| **Automatically changes** | Can go idle automatically | Never changes automatically |

You can have an Online status of "Busy" with a custom status of "in a meeting 🤫", both coexist.

## Custom Status Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Should I always have a custom status?">
    Not necessarily, many people leave it blank and it's totally fine. Custom status is a casual, expressive feature. Use it when you feel like it, not as an obligation.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How long should a custom status be?">
    Short is better. A custom status should be readable at a glance, one line, maybe 5-15 words. Think of it as a caption, not a paragraph.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can my custom status include links?">
    The custom status is plain text, links won't be clickable. If you want to direct people somewhere, put the link in your bio instead.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Who can see my custom status?">
    Your custom status visibility follows your **Presence** privacy settings. If your presence is set to "Friends Only," only friends will see your custom status.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
