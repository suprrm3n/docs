# Your Tavrn Profile URL

Every Tavrn user has a profile URL based on their username. It's the simplest way to point someone directly to your profile.

---

## Your Profile URL

Your profile URL looks like:
```
tavrn.app/profile/yourusername
```

Just replace `yourusername` with your actual Tavrn `@username`. Anyone who visits that link lands on your profile page, where they can add you as a friend or send you a message (depending on your privacy settings).

---

## How to Share It

The most common ways people share their Tavrn profile link:

- **In your social media bio**, Drop it in your Twitter, Instagram, or Linktree
- **In a stream overlay or Twitch/YouTube about section**, Let viewers easily find you on Tavrn
- **In an email signature or business card**, Useful for creators and community builders
- **In another Tavrn room**, When someone asks for your handle, you can just drop the link

---

## Finding Your Exact Username

Your `@username` is shown:
- In your profile settings (**Settings → My Account**)
- Under your display name on your own profile card
- At the top of the **Friends** list next to your avatar

---

## Username vs. Display Name

Your URL is based on your **username** (`@handle`), not your display name. These can be different:

| | Example | Used in URL? |
|---|---|---|
| **Username** | `@margaux_builds` | ✅ Yes |
| **Display Name** | `Margaux ☕ building things` | ❌ No |

If you change your username, your profile URL changes too. Old links to your previous username won't work.

---

## Privacy and Profile Links

Sharing your profile URL doesn't bypass your privacy settings. If your profile visibility is set to **Friends Only** or **Private**, people who visit your link will see a limited view, just your name and avatar, not your full profile details.

<Info>
  To control what's visible on your profile, see [Profile Visibility](/privacy-and-safety/profile-visibility).
</Info>
