# Online Status & Presence

Your status indicator is the little colored dot next to your avatar. It tells your friends and room members whether you're around and what kind of mood you're in.

---

## Setting Your Status

1. Click the **status dot** next to your avatar in the bottom of the sidebar
2. Select **Online**, **Away**, or **Busy** from the menu
3. It updates instantly

---

## Automatic Status Changes

Tavrn can automatically update your status based on activity:

- **Online Γזע Away/Idle**, After a period of inactivity (no keyboard/mouse activity), Tavrn may set you to Away automatically
- **Away Γזע Online**, When you come back and start interacting, status resets to Online

You can always override this by manually setting your status.

## Presence Privacy

You control who can see your presence status. Go to **Settings Γזע Privacy & Safety Γזע Online Status**:

- **Everyone**, All Tavrn users can see your status dot
- **Friends Only**, Only your friends can see your status dot
- **Nobody**, Your status is hidden from everyone (you always appear offline to others, even if you're actively online)

<Tip>
  If you set presence to "Nobody," you'll appear offline to everyone, even close friends. Consider "Friends Only" if you want privacy from strangers but not from people you know.
</Tip>

## Status in Friend Lists

In your friends list, your friends are sorted by their status, online friends appear at the top, offline at the bottom. This makes it easy to see at a glance who's around to chat.

Friends' status dots are visible everywhere you see their avatar, in the friends list, in room member lists, in the DM sidebar.

---

## Status in Room Member Lists

When you open a room, the member list on the right shows all members grouped by status:

1. **Online** members at the top
2. **Away/Idle** members next
3. **Busy** members below
4. **Offline** members at the bottom

<Note>
  Members who have their presence set to "Nobody" will appear in the Offline group even if they're actively online. This is intentional, they've chosen to be invisible.
</Note>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Will people know if I'm reading their messages while set to Offline/Invisible?">
    Read receipts in DMs are based on the message being opened, not your status. If you read a DM while invisible, the read receipt may still show. Your status dot will still show as offline.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does Busy (DND) silence all notifications?">
    Busy mode reduces notification prominence, you won't get audio alerts. But messages still arrive and you'll see unread indicators. It's a softer version of going invisible.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I set a status to expire after a certain time?">
    Not currently, statuses are persistent until you change them. If you set a custom status while traveling, remember to clear it when you're back.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do bots or room accounts have statuses?">
    Bot accounts and service accounts don't have a standard presence system. They typically appear offline in member lists.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
