# Profile Visibility

Your profile is your public identity on Tavrn. But "public" doesn't have to mean visible to absolutely everyone. Here's how to tune it.

---

## The Profile Visibility Setting

Go to **Settings → Privacy & Safety → Profile** to choose one of three levels:

### 🌍 Everyone
Your full profile is visible to everyone on Tavrn, friends, strangers, anyone who looks you up.

This includes:
- Your display name, username, and avatar
- Your bio and pronouns
- Your banner color
- Your Steam connection (if connected)
- Your Cocoa badge (if you have one)
- Mutual friends and rooms (for people who share them with you)

### 👥 Friends Only
Only people on your friends list can see your full profile details. To everyone else, your profile shows a limited view, typically just your name and avatar.

### 🔒 Only Me
Your profile details are hidden from everyone, including people who aren't your friends. Only the bare minimum (display name, avatar) is visible when you show up in rooms or conversations.

---

## What's Always Visible, Regardless of Setting

Even on Only Me, a few things are always visible:

- **Your display name**, People need to know who they're talking to in rooms
- **Your avatar**, Shows up next to your messages
- **Your username**, Used for @mentions

These can't be hidden because hiding them would break basic functionality.

---

## Profile Visibility vs. Other Privacy Settings

Profile visibility is just one piece. It works together with other settings:

| Setting | What it controls |
|---|---|
| **Profile Visibility** | Who can see your bio, social links, etc. |
| **Friend List Visibility** | Who can see your friends list specifically |
| **Presence / Status** | Who can see your online status dot |
| **Search Visibility** | Whether you appear in search results |

These are all configured separately in **Settings → Privacy & Safety**, and they compound. For example, you could have a public profile but hide your friends list, or have a private profile but still be searchable by username.

---

## The Friends-Only Sweet Spot

For most people, **Friends Only** hits the right balance:

- People you've connected with see the full picture
- Strangers see just enough to decide if they want to send a friend request
- Your personal details (bio, social links, location) aren't exposed to everyone

If you're a creator or community figure who wants to be discoverable, **Everyone** makes more sense. If you just want privacy from everyone who isn't explicitly your friend, **Only Me** is there for you.

---

## How Profile Visibility Interacts with Rooms

When you're a member of a public room, people in that room can see your name and avatar in the member list regardless of your profile visibility setting. Your *profile details* (bio, social links) remain gated by your privacy setting, just your presence in the room is visible.

---

## Mutual Content

When two people share mutual friends or rooms, those connections are shown on each other's profiles, but only to the extent each person's privacy settings allow.

If your profile is set to Only Me, mutual friends/rooms won't be shown on your profile when someone else views it.

---

## Changing Your Visibility

1. Go to **Settings → Privacy & Safety**
2. Find **Profile Visibility**
3. Select your preferred option
4. Changes take effect immediately, no save button needed

<Note>
  Profile visibility changes apply to anyone viewing your profile going forward. There's no cache to worry about, it's live instantly.
</Note>
